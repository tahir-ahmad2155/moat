Case Study 1: Asset Restructuring & Consolidation

Individual Investor | Multi-Broker Asset Consolidation

Client Snapshot

Profile

  • Individual investor with scattered and overdiversified portfolio
  • Assets accumulated over multiple years

Asset Spread

  • Holdings across multiple brokers and platforms
  • Physical share certificates (paper shares)
  • Mix of equity investments and mutual funds
  • Demat and non-demat holdings

Key Issue

  • Lost contact points with several brokers
  • Name discrepancies in client database Records
  • Lack unified portfolio visibility

The Challenge

  • Assets are scattered across different brokers, with lost points of contact
  • Name errors and inconsistencies in the client database across platforms
  • Physical share certificates requiring dematerialisation
  • No consolidated view of the total portfolio
  • Difficulty in tracking performance and managing assets effectively
  • KYC compliance issues due to name mismatches

The Msuite Approach

Asset Consolidation

  • Re-established contact with all brokers
  • Consolidated financial assets from multiple sources and built a character for the portfolio
  • Dematerialised physical share certificates

KYC Completion & Compliance

  • Completed KYC process on behalf of the client
  • Resolved name discrepancies across all platforms
  • Standardised client information across brokers
